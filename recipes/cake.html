Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Baking soda
  • Food colouring
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175C and grease and flour a 9-inch pan
  2. Cream sugar and sugar in a mixing bowl. Add eggs one at a time while whisking vanilla extract.
  3. Combine flower and baking powder in a seperate bowl. Add to wet ingredients and mix well.
  4. Pour batter in prepared pan tray
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until the top springs slightly when touched, which is 30 to 40 minutes typically
  6. Remove from oven and cool completely

    7. And Voila! Frost and enjoy!
