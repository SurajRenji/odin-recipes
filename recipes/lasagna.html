Lasagna

Home Page

In this classic lasagna recipe, sheets of pasta are layered with a cheesy filling, a rich meaty tomato sauce, and more cheese and then baked until bubbly and browned.

Ingredients

  • Cheese filling
  • Meat
  • Sauce
  • Spinach
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Brown the meat and onions
  3. Prepare the baking dish and assemble the sauce
  4. Begin layering the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna for 1 hour
  6. Sprinkle the cheese and finish baking
  7. Cool the lasagna for 10 minutes

And Voila!
