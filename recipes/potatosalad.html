Potato Salad

Home Page

Potato salad is a popular dish made from boiled potatoes, typically mixed with a dressing, and often including other ingredients like hard-boiled eggs, celery, onions, and pickles. It's commonly served as a side dish, especially during summer barbecues and picnics. The dressing often consists of mayonnaise, mustard, and seasonings like salt and pepper

Ingredients

  • potatoes
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard

    • Optional

  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Pickles
  • Vinegar
